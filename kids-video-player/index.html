Support & Privacy

Kids Videos: Parent Picks

A parent-managed video library app for families. Parents approve the YouTube videos and playlists first, then children watch from those approved choices.

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Support

For help with Kids Videos: Parent Picks, visit the support issue tracker at github.com/MindCoreAI/kids-video-player/issues.

Privacy Policy

Kids Videos: Parent Picks is designed to be local-first. The app stores family settings on the device wherever possible and does not currently include developer-operated analytics.

Information Stored On Your Device

  • Kid profiles are stored locally on the device.
  • Approved video and playlist libraries are stored locally on the device.
  • Parent passcode hashes and recovery material are stored locally.
  • Backup export and import use local files that you choose to create or open.

YouTube Services

The app uses the YouTube Data API for video and playlist information and embedded YouTube playback to show videos. Use of YouTube services is subject to Google and YouTube privacy policies and terms, including the Google Privacy Policy, the YouTube Terms of Service, and the YouTube API Services Terms of Service.

Kids Videos: Parent Picks does not download videos and does not provide offline video playback. Videos play through YouTube's embedded playback experience.

Analytics And Tracking

The app does not currently send developer analytics, advertising analytics, or usage tracking data to the developer.

Purchases And Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple may process purchase, subscription, billing, and refund information under Apple's own policies.

Deleting Data

Uninstalling the app removes local app data from the device. Any backup files you exported separately remain wherever you saved them unless you delete those files yourself.

Terms Of Use

Kids Videos: Parent Picks is provided for personal family use. Parents and guardians are responsible for choosing which videos and playlists are appropriate for their children.

The app depends on YouTube availability, YouTube account or API access where applicable, network connectivity, and videos remaining available from their publishers. Videos, metadata, thumbnails, and playback may change or become unavailable outside the developer's control.

Do not use the app in a way that violates Apple, Google, or YouTube terms, or any applicable law.
