Support & Privacy

Family Tree & Branchline

An ancestry and kinship mapping app for building, viewing, and maintaining family trees on iPhone and iPad.

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Support

For help, app feedback, privacy questions, or deletion requests, use the support issue tracker at github.com/MindCoreAI/family-tree-app/issues.

Platform iPhone and iPad
Bundle ID com.codex.familytree

Privacy Policy

Family Tree & Branchline is designed to keep family-tree content on your device. The developer does not currently operate an account system, cloud sync service, or developer analytics service for this app.

Information Stored On Your Device

  • Family trees, people, relationships, notes, dates, places, and app settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Photos you add to profiles are stored locally as app data.
  • Backup import and export use local JSON files that you choose to open, save, or share.

Advertising And Consent

The app uses Google Mobile Ads to show banner ads and Google User Messaging Platform to support regional consent and privacy options. Google may process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, diagnostics, ad interaction, and related data according to Google's policies and your consent choices.

Learn more in the Google Privacy Policy. You can use the app's Ad Privacy Options when they are required for your region.

In-App Purchases

Family Tree & Branchline offers a one-time Remove Ads purchase. In-app purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple may process purchase, billing, refund, and transaction information under Apple's own policies.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your family-tree data. Files exported from the app remain under your control, and sharing exported files is your choice.

Deleting Data

You can delete family data in the app or uninstall the app to remove local app data from the device. Any backup files you exported separately remain wherever you saved them unless you delete those files yourself.

Terms Of Use

Family Tree & Branchline is provided for personal family-history organization. You are responsible for the accuracy of information you enter and for obtaining permission before sharing another person's private family information.

Do not use the app in a way that violates Apple, Google, or AdMob terms, or any applicable law.
